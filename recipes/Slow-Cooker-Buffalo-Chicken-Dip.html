Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Dip

About this recipe

“Get ready to meet your new snack obsession: Buffalo chicken dip! Toss raw chicken into your slow cooker, shred it with a hand mixer, stir in the magic mix of ingredients, and four hours later…boom! Creamy, spicy, cheesy goodness ready for dunking.”

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese (Neufchatel)
  • 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
  • ½ cup Buffalo wing sauce (such as Frank's® Red Hot)
  • ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Place in the slow cooker and cover with the lid.
  2. Cook on Low until chicken is cooked through, about 3 hours. Drain accumulated juices. Shred chicken using a hand mixer. Add cream cheese, sour cream, Buffalo wing sauce, and Cheddar cheese. Cook on Low for 1 more hour. Stir to combine.

Tips

  • For hassle-free cleanup, coat the slow cooker with a light layer of nonstick spray.
  • Skip the scrubbing—just pop in a slow cooker liner before cooking.
